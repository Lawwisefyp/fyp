Mr. Ali entered into a contract with Mr. Hassan on 10 January 2025 for the supply of office furniture worth Rs. 500,000. 
Advance payment of Rs. 200,000 was made. The remaining amount was to be paid upon delivery.

The delivery was scheduled for 10 February 2025. 
Mr. Hassan failed to deliver the furniture and did not respond to repeated reminders.

Mr. Ali suffered business losses due to non-delivery.
